Privacy Policy

Privacy-First Approach

TagRigger is a standalone data manager. We believe that your data is yours. The application runs locally on your machine and does not collect, transmit, or store any of your tags, catalogs, or usage data on external servers.

License Key Verification

If you purchase a PRO version, the application may securely communicate with our servers (or our reseller's servers) solely for the purpose of validating your license key. No personal data other than what is necessary for license verification is transmitted.

Payment Information

All payments are securely processed by our authorized reseller, PayPro Global. We do not collect or store your credit card details or billing information. Please refer to PayPro Global's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment data.

Contact
